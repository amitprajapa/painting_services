Painting Process


1. Sanitization: We care for you!! so the first step is we make sure that all our crew members , Products & Tools are completely sanitize.
2. Covering and preparation: we take care of each and everything. We start covering the furniture, sofa, tables, switchboards and other elements like TV, fans, etc.
3. Surface Preparation: Scrapping, Removing loose paints, cracking etc
4. Two Coat Putty: Putty is a white cement that is applied to the drywalls and ceilings. With the help of the putty we can fill the pores and make the surface smooth.
5. Priming: Primer ensures better adhesion of the paint to the surface to increase paint durability and create a uniform appearancePainting: After the primer is dried, the painting process is started.
6. Usually, 2 coats of paints are required, but colors with high sheen may require more than that. Coloring gives a final touch up to the wall and makes it look gorgeous.

What procedure do you follow while painting the interior of house?

As a professional service provider, we thoroughly check the condition of the walls and ceiling for any dampness, seepage or chipping off. If the current condition is not good, we recommend sanding of surface then 2 coats putty a coat of primer and 2 coats of paint. And if the condition is good, we suggest sanding, A coat of primer and 2 coats of paint.

How do you take care of the furniture and other things of the house?

Before starting the painting procedure, our team would pack the fixed furniture and mask all the things with plastic sheets and newspaper based on the availability. After the completion of work, we do a dry dusting and ensure no paint stains are left over. We offer this hassle-free service at no extra cost.

Do you charge per sqft? how do you calculate the total paintable area?

Yes, we practice a genuine pricing methodology. We make sure either of us must not be at loss. We calculate only the paintable surface and factor it out based on the products selected by the customers and arrive at the total cost. In this process, we consider the height/Length/Width of the space and consider the only area that can be painted. Means, we do remove areas covered by Fixed furniture, cabinets, wall claddings, Tiles and also segregate wooden and metal into different scope of work and arrive at the final cost. Also, we use Laser technology based measuring equipment for the accurate and hassle free measurement process. Our service starts at as low as Rs. 6/- per sq ft.
