Painting Process

1) High pressure washing

The process of exterior painting begins with high pressure washing. This helps to remove all the dirt, dust particles from the wall.

2) Primer

The second step is of applying primer. It improves the adhesion of paint to the wall.

3) Crack Filling

Crack seal is used to fill the cracks on the exterior wall. This prevents the water seepage.

4) Two coats on paints

Two coats of paint are applied on the wall one after the other. Once those are completed you can admire your perfectly painted walls!!!
